Transforming Your Dreams into Reality

I’m Cheryl Terry, a passionate and inspiring transformation coach dedicated to helping you to create your best life — the life you deserve.

My coaching practice is built upon a strong foundation of experience. I worked for 25 years in education, and for the past 8 years, I have been working as a professional coach. I bring extensive experience in coaching conversations and am accredited as a Transformation Life Coach, Professional Life Coach and Master Life Coach, as well as being a trained Cognitive Coach.

I don't just teach transformation — I live it. I personally transformed my life at a pivotal time, successfully semi-retiring at the age of 46 and retiring at 51 years of age. I am living proof of the power of intention, as I learned to set intentions and manifest my dreams.

Now, I want to empower others to transform their dreams into reality. Let’s start the journey to creating your best life together.
